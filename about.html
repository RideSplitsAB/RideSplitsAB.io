Reimagine Your Commute. Connect, Save, and Go Green.

RideSplits is transforming the way we think about transportation. Born from a need for equitable, affordable, and sustainable commuting, our platform connects individuals traveling similar routes so they can share rides, reduce costs, and build community. Unlike traditional rideshare companies, RideSplits is built for everyday people not professional drivers. It’s a peer-to-peer network that empowers commuters to carpool along their regular paths, turning empty seats into real savings and real impact.
We believe that access to efficient and affordable transportation is a human right. Whether you’re a student, a worker, or anyone trying to get around, RideSplits gives you a better way to move without needing to own a car. Fewer cars, more community, and a healthier planet.
