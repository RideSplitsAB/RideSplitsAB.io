Driven by Purpose. United by Innovation.

RideSplits was founded by a passionate team of students, engineers, and community advocates determined to solve the transportation challenges facing regions like California’s Inland Empire. Grounded in research and lived experience, our team understands the daily struggles of long commutes, high car costs, and unreliable public transit.
We’re committed to equity, sustainability, and innovation. From building tech solutions that scale across cities to forging partnerships with local governments and businesses, our team works tirelessly to make carpooling a mainstream, dependable mode of transportation for everyone.
