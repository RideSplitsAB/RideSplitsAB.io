A Smarter Way to Ride Together

Our app is designed for simplicity, safety, and impact. Drivers can set their own fares, making rides competitive and affordable, while passengers easily book seats through a streamlined interface. With features like ride tracking, emergency contact alerts, and future plans for women-centered ride options (4WomanRides), safety and comfort are built in.
RideSplits also rewards users for sustainable travel. Through our Carpool Rewards Program, frequent users unlock perks like discounts on gas, auto services, and even FastTrack passes. We’re not just connecting rides we’re building a culture of shared mobility.
Download the app today and start splitting your ride, your cost, and your carbon footprint.
